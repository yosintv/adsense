# Sustainable Tech: The Rise of Green Streaming

As the demand for 8K streaming grew, so did the energy consumption of the internet. In 2026, the tech industry has responded with a series of innovations aimed at making our digital lives more sustainable.

## Efficient Codecs

New video compression standards allow for higher quality with 50% less data, reducing the energy needed for transmission across the globe.

## Solar-Powered Data Centers

Major streaming providers now run their server farms on 100% renewable energy, with many even contributing excess power back to the local grid.

## Eco-Hardware

From recyclable remotes to TVs made from ocean-bound plastics, the hardware we use to watch is finally catching up with our environmental values.

## Conclusion

Streaming doesn't have to cost the earth. Through innovation and commitment, 2026 is the year we made entertainment sustainable.
