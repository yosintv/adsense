# Cloud Gaming: How It Became the Netflix for Gamers

The year 2026 has seen the final transition from physical consoles to pure cloud-based gaming. Why buy a $600 box when you can stream 4K, 120FPS games to your TV, tablet, or even your smart fridge?

## Low Latency: The Final Barrier

The rollout of 6G and the massive expansion of edge computing nodes have reduced latency to the point where even competitive pro-gamers can play in the cloud without feeling a delay.

## Subscription Over Ownership

Just like movies and music, gaming has moved to a subscription model. For a flat monthly fee, gamers get access to thousands of titles instantly.

## Cross-Device Seamlessness

Start playing a game on your 100-inch MicroLED TV at home, and continue exactly where you left off on your phone during your commute. The cloud makes hardware irrelevant.

## Conclusion

Gaming has been democratized. In 2026, all you need is a screen and a fast connection to enjoy the world's most demanding games.
