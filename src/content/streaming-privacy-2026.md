# How to Protect Your Privacy While Streaming: 2026 Checklist

Smart TVs are some of the most data-hungry devices in our homes. In 2026, the amount of data they collect—from what you watch to what's in your room via built-in cameras—is staggering.

## Disabling ACR (Automated Content Recognition)

This is the technology that "watches" what's on your screen and sends the data back to advertisers. We show you how to find and disable it on LG, Samsung, and Sony TVs.

## App Permissions

Just like on your phone, streaming apps often ask for more data than they need. We show you how to audit your app permissions and limit tracking.

## Using a Dedicated Streaming Box

Often, using a device like an Apple TV or a privacy-focused Android box is safer than using the built-in OS of your smart TV.

## Conclusion

Privacy is a right, not a luxury. By taking a few minutes to configure your settings, you can enjoy your content without being the product.
