# How to Stream Region-Locked Content Without a VPN

While VPNs are popular, they can sometimes trigger blocks on major streaming platforms. In 2026, many savvy cord-cutters are turning to alternative methods to access content from other countries.

## Smart DNS

Smart DNS only changes your DNS settings for specific streaming domains, meaning your actual internet speed remains unaffected. It's often faster than a VPN and harder for services to detect.

## Residential Proxies

By routing your traffic through real residential IP addresses rather than data center IPs, these proxies are almost impossible for streaming platforms to block.

## Global Accounts

Some services now offer "Global Accounts" that allow you to switch regions within the app settings for an extra fee, bypassing the need for any technical workarounds.

## Conclusion

The wall between countries is falling, but you still need the right tools to navigate the digital world.
